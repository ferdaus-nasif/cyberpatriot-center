Peshrow's Page

⌃

Where do I even start? Peshrow Khosravi is a senior and a very valuable member of Team 6. He brought lots of positivity on the team, and we were so glad to have him with us. He was an amazing individual, not just in CyberPatriot, but in the outside world. I still remember the day I met him. I got paired with him to do some crypto assignment in Cyber. Ever since then, we just clicked, and we started talking, and we still do to this day. Team 6 really appreciates the effort Peshrow put in on this team. Specifically, during Round 2 when Peshrow did Server. Even though he didn't know much, he still put in work, getting help from friends like Rushil Ramkumar, who also did Server. Peshrow had a great Indian friend named Adi Godugu (aka Audas 66). He motivated him to do his best and lock in and work and play hard and forget about what Chhabra done to him. Although he has neutral relationships with some people (Laroussi), nonetheless he is a great person. One memorable moment was when during the Semifinals he ordered Chick-fil-A and ate it because no one knew what they were doing, and they thought they were genuinely going to lose the semifinals (they did). There was also a time during Semifinals where me, Peshrow, Laroussi, and Adi played Wii Party on Dolphin Emulator. Very fun time we had. We also joke about Peshrow getting into colleges like Virginia Tech and Pitt (University of Pittsburgh). Anyway, we wish Peshrow a happy graduation from Indy, and best of luck moving forward. And to Peshrow, congrats on your first and last CyberPatriot. We will remember you, friend.

Thank you, Peshrow
